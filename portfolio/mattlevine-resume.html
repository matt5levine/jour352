What Do I Do?

I am the Director of Broadcasting and Media at the Maryland Baseball Network and the Station Director at WMUC Sports Radio. I am also a play-by-play broadcaster that has experience across several sports. I consider myself a very versatile announcer who is always available and will work for success.
